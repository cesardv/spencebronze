About Us

When you experience the loss of a loved one, you deserve personal, professional, and caring service. We work with you to design a beautiful memorial that will reflect the special story of a life well lived and lovingly remembered.

We create extraordinary and impressive bronze and stone signs for businesses, public facilities, churches, temples, universities, and schools. We also provide bronze plaques for sculptures and for recognition and commemorative products.

We are a family owned business, at the same location, proudly serving the community for over three-quarters of a century. Spence Monument Company enjoys a proud reputation for taking care of families for generations. We take the time required to provide personal and compassionate service.

Our Matthews Bronze Markers and vases are recognized throughout the world as the leading manufacturer of cast bronze products since 1850. They are truly handcrafted works of art.
